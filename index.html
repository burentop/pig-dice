Play the game of Pig

Enter the names of the 2 players and then press 'Play'.

Game play: When it is your turn, click roll and then the die number is added to your Round Score.

You may roll as many times as you want, and your Round Score will continue to increase.

The Round Score is not added to your Final Score until you click 'Hold', ending your turn.

If you roll a '1' your turn ends with Zero points added to your Final Score. First player to 100 wins the game.
